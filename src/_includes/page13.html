In the case of the analog photoshoot, on the day of the photoshoot we organized each step of the day:

  • Picking up furniture
  • Getting authorizations to bring furniture to the studio
  • Welcoming the models and making sure they had everything they needed
  • Meeting with the photographer and styling assistant before the shoot starts
  • Set-up the studio with props and organise a changing area, a food and rest area
  • Get the clothes of the collection ready on a rack in order for each scene.
